Commodity / Oil Shock — Never Get Caught Again

You got hurt on the oil drop. That's fixable with a systematic rules set. Oil ($57 → $90+ from Iran) and the sudden reversal ($97 → collapse on ceasefire) are both predictable IF you watch the right signals. No news required.

Oil Long Setup — Geopolitical Premium Signals
01
OVX (Oil VIX) term structure — when OVX spikes and goes backwardated, oil is pricing in a fear premium. That premium EXISTS until the fear event resolves. Hold long until resolution.
02
Speculative positioning (COT report) — CFTC Commitments of Traders, published every Friday. When managed money is at multi-year max long: don't add. When near max short: setup brewing.
03
Physical supply signal — Strait of Hormuz transits (tracked by Kpler, Vortexa). Transit volume near ZERO = premium holds. ANY reopening = sell trigger immediately.
04
Crude oil futures curve (contango vs backwardation) — Backwardation in WTI = physical tightness = hold longs. Curve flips to contango = physical pressure easing = reduce.
05
Energy sector dark pool — Watch for institutional dark pool exits in XLE, XOM, CVX. Large block sells at bid = smart money distributing into geopolitical fear. That is your exit signal.
Rules to Never Get Wrecked on Oil Reversal
R1
Geopolitical premium has a binary unwind risk. Set a hard rule: any ceasefire/resolution headline = reduce 50% immediately at market. Then reassess. Never argue with the binary event.
R2
Price-stop at OVX collapse. When OVX drops 15%+ intraday, the risk premium is LEAVING. That is a mechanical exit trigger regardless of your fundamental view.
R3
Never size oil for gap risk without defined exit. Oil moved -14% in a single session on April 8. Position size such that a full -15% gap overnight doesn't blow your account. Use defined-risk options if sizing large.
R4
Watch Strait transit data weekly. Tools: Kpler (paid), Bloomberg shipping. The physical data tells you BEFORE the ceasefire headline what's happening. Transits rising = anticipate headline resolution.
R5
Hedge with equity inverse correlation. When long oil for geopolitical premium, the hedge is: long equities / SPX calls. The ceasefire that kills your oil position RIPS equities. April 8 proved this perfectly.
